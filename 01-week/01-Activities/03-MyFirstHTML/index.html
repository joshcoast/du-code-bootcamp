Mr. Sandwich

  1. Lay two slices of bread side by side on a plate.
  2. Place a slice of ham on the left slice of bread.
  3. Squeeze a little bit of mustard onto that same slice ham.
  4. Place the right slice of bread on top of the left slice of bread, ham, and mustard.
  5. Done.
